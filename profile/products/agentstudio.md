## What it is · 这是什么

A no-code macOS workbench. You describe what you want in plain language; two AI agents collaborate — one plans, one builds and self-checks — iterating until you have something that actually runs.
<br><sub>一个不用写代码的 macOS 工作台。你用大白话说出想要什么，两个 AI 协作 —— 一个做规划、一个动手并自检 —— 反复迭代，直到做出能真正跑起来的东西。</sub>

## Why it exists · 为什么做它

Turning an idea into a working tool usually means writing code. AgentStudio removes that step for small, personal tools.
<br><sub>把一个点子变成能用的工具，通常意味着写代码。AgentStudio 把这一步省掉了，专为小而个人的工具而生。</sub>

## How it works · 怎么运作

- **You describe** the outcome you want, in plain words
- **The planner agent** breaks it into steps
- **The builder agent** executes and **self-checks** each step
- **It loops** until the result works — then it's yours

## Who it's for · 适合谁

- Non-coders with ideas they want to make real
- Tinkerers who want the fastest path from idea → tool
