## What it is · 这是什么

A reader built for Markdown and HTML, local-first — your files live on your device, not in a cloud you don't control. Preview them cleanly, edit inline, export to print-ready PDF, and pull an AI summary only when you ask for one.
<br><sub>一款专读 Markdown 与 HTML 的阅读器，本地优先 —— 文件留在你设备上，而不是某个你管不到的云。干净预览、就地编辑、导出 PDF，AI 摘要只在你点的时候才用。</sub>

## Why it exists · 为什么做它

Most readers either lock your notes into a cloud or render Markdown badly. MuseView keeps your files yours and your reading clean.
<br><sub>多数阅读器要么把笔记锁进云端，要么把 Markdown 渲染得很糟。MuseView 让文件归你、让阅读清爽。</sub>

## Features · 功能

- **Markdown & HTML** with faithful rendering
- **Local-first** — files stay on-device
- **Live preview** with inline editing
- **Export** to clean, print-ready PDF
- **AI summaries on demand** — only when you tap

## Who it's for · 适合谁

- Heavy readers, note-takers, and researchers
- Anyone with a folder of `.md` files they actually open
