## What Sigilo is

Sigilo is a privacy-first two-factor authentication (2FA) app that generates the one-time codes — **TOTP and HOTP** — you need to sign in securely to Google, GitHub, Microsoft, Dropbox, AWS, and any service that supports standard authenticator apps.

It's built around a single idea: **your data belongs to you.** Codes live on your device by default, and only ever leave it when *you* turn on sync.

## Local-first by default

The codes you add stay on your iPhone. If you never turn on sync, **nothing ever leaves your device** — no account required, no cloud to trust.

Need your codes on more than one device, or a safety net when you switch phones? Turn on **optional cloud sync** with Apple, Google, or GitHub. It's always under your control and only runs when you ask it to.

## Everything you expect from an authenticator

| | |
|---|---|
| 🔑 **TOTP & HOTP** | Works with any service that supports standard authenticator apps. |
| ➕ **Three ways to add** | Scan a QR code, paste an `otpauth` link, or enter a key by hand. |
| 🗂️ **Folders & search** | Organize with folders and find any code instantly. |
| 🆔 **Face ID lock** | A Face ID / passcode lock keeps your codes for your eyes only. |
| 💾 **Encrypted backup** | Export an encrypted backup file you can restore yourself. |
| ☁️ **Optional sync** | End-to-end, cross-device, off by default — entirely your choice. |

## Privacy you can trust

- **No ads, ever**
- **No third-party tracking or analytics SDKs**
- **We never sell your data**
- **Delete your account anytime in-app** — it permanently removes your account and any synced data from our servers
