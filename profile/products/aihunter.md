## What it is · 这是什么

A curated directory of AI tools — updated every day and sorted by what you're trying to get done. Instead of scrolling another "top 100 AI tools" list, you get a small, vetted set worth actually trying.
<br><sub>一个每天更新、按使用场景归类的 AI 工具目录。不用再刷「100 个必备 AI 工具」那种长列表，这里只给你一小撮真正值得一试的。</sub>

## Why it exists · 为什么做它

New AI tools appear faster than anyone can keep up with, and most "lists" are SEO filler. We read, try, and categorize them so you don't have to.
<br><sub>AI 工具出现的速度没人跟得上，多数榜单只是 SEO 灌水。我们替你看、替你试、替你归类。</sub>

## Features · 功能

- **Daily picks** — a steady trickle, not a firehose
- **Sorted by use case** — writing, image, video, code, audio, productivity…

- **Each entry** tells you what it does, who it's for, and links straight to it

## Who it's for · 适合谁
