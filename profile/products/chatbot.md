## What it is · 这是什么

AI companions that live right inside WeChat. Choose from 50+ personas, scan a QR code to add one, and chat anytime — no separate app to download. Each companion keeps track of your conversation, so chats feel continuous.
<br><sub>住在微信里的 AI 陪伴。50+ 人设任你挑，扫码加上就能随时聊，不用另外装 app。每个人设都会记得你们聊过什么，对话有连续感。</sub>

## Why it exists · 为什么做它

Talking to an AI usually means opening yet another app. AI Companions meets you where you already are — WeChat — with a character you actually like.
